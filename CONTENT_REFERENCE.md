# Content Reference: COMERS BBQ CATFISH

## Category: Restaurant

### Services
#### Fresh Catfish Daily

Our catfish is sourced fresh daily, hand-battered and fried to golden perfection. Nothing beats genuine Southern-fried catfish.
**Features:** Filets sized to order | Crispy cornmeal batter | House-made tartar sauce

#### Traditional BBQ Ribs

Slow-smoked over hickory wood for 12+ hours until tender. Our signature rub and homemade sauce deliver authentic Texas BBQ flavor.
**Features:** Full and half racks | Dry rub or sauced | Pulled pork sandwiches

#### Southern Comfort Food

From fried chicken to meat-and-three plates, we serve hearty Southern favorites made with traditional recipes.
**Features:** Daily lunch specials | Fried okra and sides | Homemade desserts

#### Catering & Events

Planning a party? We cater events of all sizes, from birthday dinners to corporate functions. Call ahead for custom menus.
**Features:** Full meal packages | Buffet-style service | Family meal deals

### About Story
Comer's BBQ and Catfish has been a Carthage favorite for three generations, starting when Granddaddy Comer first opened his little fry shack in 1974. Today we are proud to serve the community that raised us, maintaining those same family recipes and welcoming every guest like extended family.
